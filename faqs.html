What measures do you take to ensure the authenticity and quality of artworks?

Before we even considered selling artworks, we've spoken to multiple printing houses and their corresponding paper expert.

We've collected as much information as needed that are related to the quality of paper and how the very process of painting/printing on a specific type of paper is done. Next we've done some experiments on these types of papers to ensure that the given information were as accurate as they could be.

Finally we've experimented with multiple painting brands, whether they are specified in acrylic paintings, oil paintings, gouache paintings or other types of paintings.
Thus, before adding an artwork to the website, we check these parameters, and, if they comply with our requirements, they are welcomed.

What is your return and refund policy?

Return and Refund Policy
At Artful Atlas, we want you to be completely satisfied with your artwork purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

Returns
You may return any artwork within the day of receiving it for a full refund or exchange in the following cases :
+ You've received the wrong artwork.
+ The artwork is damaged (in some rare cases where the shipping medium stumbles.)

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team via Contact to request a return authorization. Returned artworks must be in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging intact.

Refunds
Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned artwork. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 3 days.

Damaged or Defective Items
If your artwork arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately with photographic evidence. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as soon as possible.

Exchanges

Contact Us
If you have any questions about our return and refund policy, or if you need assistance with a return, please contact our customer service team via contact.
