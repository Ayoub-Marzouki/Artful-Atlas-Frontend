What measures do you take to ensure the authenticity and quality of artworks?

Before we even considered selling artworks, we've spoken to multiple printing houses and their corresponding paper expert. We've collected as much information as needed that are related to the quality of paper and how the very process of painting/printing on a specific type of paper is done. Next we've done some experiments on these types of papers to ensure that the given information were as accurate as they could be. Finally we've experimented with multiple painting brands, whether they are specified in acrylic paintings, oil paintings, gouache paintings or other types of paintings.
Thus, before adding an artwork to the website, we check these parameters, and, if they comply with our requirements, they are welcomed.

How can I contact customer support for assistance?

Visiting our contact page, you're going to find our emails that are specific to helping our customers. Feel free to ask about anything anytime! Time response currently does not surpass 8 hours in worst cases.

What is your return and refund policy?

Return and Refund Policy
At Artful Atlas, we want you to be completely satisfied with your artwork purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.
Returns
You may return any artwork within the day of receiving it for a full refund or exchange in the following cases :
+ You've received the wrong artwork (which is very unlikely to happen).
+ The artwork is damaged (in some rare cases where the shipping medium stumbles.)
To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team via Contact to request a return authorization. Returned artworks must be in their original condition, unused, and with all original packaging intact.
Refunds
Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned artwork. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 3 days.
Damaged or Defective Items
If your artwork arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately with photographic evidence. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as soon as possible.
Shipping Costs
Exchanges
If you wish to exchange your artwork for a different piece, please contact us to arrange for an exchange.
Contact Us
If you have any questions about our return and refund policy, or if you need assistance with a return, please contact our customer service team via contact.

Who are we?

Artful Atlas is an online platform on the surface. Deep inside on the other hand, it's a vibrant community dedicated to showcasing the rich Moroccan artistry to the world. Our mission is to amplify the essence of Moroccan identity by spotlighting emerging talents who skillfully capture the essence of their heritage through their distinctive artistic expressions.
Ever pondered the possibility of communication transcending linguistic barriers? We've discovered a universal language; that is art. Through the powerful medium of artistic expression, we break down language barriers and connect individuals from diverse backgrounds on a profound level. Join us in celebrating the boundless communication and understanding fostered by the language of art. Through our curated selection of artwork, we aim to bridge cultures and foster a deeper appreciation for the beauty and diversity of Moroccan art. Our team is passionate about supporting young Moroccan artists on their journey to global recognition. We provide a platform for these talents to shine, offering them exposure and opportunities to showcase their work to a global audience. Join us on our journey as we celebrate the creativity, innovation, and cultural richness of Morocco, one masterpiece at a time.

Our mission and values

Our mission is deeply rooted in promoting Moroccan artistry and cultural heritage to the world. We hold these core values close to our hearts:

  • Celebrating Moroccan identity: We are committed to showcasing the diverse and vibrant artistic expressions that capture the essence of Moroccan culture and heritage.
  • Fostering creativity and innovation: We believe in nurturing and supporting emerging talents, providing them with a platform to express their creativity and share their unique perspectives with the world.
  • Promoting cultural exchange: Through our selection of artwork, we strive to foster cross-cultural dialogue and appreciation, bridging gaps and fostering understanding among individuals from diverse backgrounds.
  • Empowering artists: We empower artists by offering them exposure, opportunities, and resources to grow and thrive in their artistic journey, ensuring that their voices are heard and their talents are recognized globally.
  • Community-driven approach: We believe in the power of community and collaboration. We aim to cultivate a supportive and inclusive community where artists and art enthusiasts come together to share their passion for art and creativity.
